Welcome to the car rescue app!

The go-to app for users if they need anything when their car has let them down! Here are three different features to select from:

  1. Self Help

    The first feature gives you step-by-step instructions for some of the typical car issues you might face.

  2. User assistance

    The second feature provides a unique solution by pinging nearby users for assistance, in case you couldn't solve the issue yourself.

  3. Professional help

    The third feature acts as a final resort and allows you to contact roadside assistance and access mechanics nearby to help your situation.
