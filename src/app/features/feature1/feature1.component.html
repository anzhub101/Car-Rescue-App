Changing a Tire

1. Loosen the lug nuts.

2. Lift the car with a jack.

3. Remove the tire and replace it with the spare.

4. Tighten the lug nuts.

Jump Starting a Battery

1. Connect the positive jumper cable to the positive terminal of the dead battery.

2. Connect the other end of the positive cable to the positive terminal of the good battery.

3. Connect the negative cable to the negative terminal of the good battery.

4. Connect the other end of the negative cable to a metal part of the car with the dead battery.

5. Start the car with the good battery, then try to start the car with the dead battery.

Adding Engine Oil

1. Open the hood and find the oil filler cap.

2. Remove the oil filler cap and add necessary amount of oil.

3. Fit the oil filler cap securely.

4. Check the oil level with the dipstick to ensure it's within the proper range.
